# I'm Alive App — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 2026*

## Overview
I'm Alive is a wellness and safety check-in application. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect
- **Account information**: Name and email address used to create your account
- **Health and wellness data**: Daily check-in status, mood, water intake, meal intake, and medication tracking
- **Contact information**: Names and phone numbers of designated contacts you add to the app

## How We Use Your Information
- To provide daily wellness check-in functionality
- To send notifications to your designated contacts when you check in or trigger an alert
- To display your personal history and streak data within the app

## Data Sharing
- Your wellness data is shared only with contacts you explicitly add and configure within the app
- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes

## Data Storage
- All data is stored securely using Google Firebase with encryption in transit and at rest
- You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen

## SMS Notifications
- SMS notifications are sent via Twilio on your behalf to contacts you have added
- Standard message and data rates may apply for recipients

## Contact
For privacy questions contact us at: support@imalive.app

## Changes
We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
