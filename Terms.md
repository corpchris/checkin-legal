# I'm Alive App — Terms and Conditions

*Last updated: April 2026*

## Program Description
I'm Alive is a wellness and safety check-in application that allows users to log their daily health status and share that information with designated contacts.

## Message & Data Rates
Standard message and data rates may apply for SMS notifications sent to your contacts.

## Message Frequency
Message frequency varies based on your check-in activity and the notification settings you configure for each contact.

## How to Get Help
For support, reply HELP to any message or contact us at support@imalive.app

## How to Opt Out
To stop receiving messages, reply STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

## Support Contact
Email: support@imalive.app

## Disclaimer
I'm Alive is not a substitute for emergency services. In case of emergency always call 911.
